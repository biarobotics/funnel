Accredited Investor Verification

For manual review under SEC Reg D. Share a secure view-only link to your docs, or email redacted files to verify@biarobotics.com.

Acceptable documentation (any one of):

  • Letter from CPA / Attorney / RIA confirming accredited status (preferred).
  • Assets > $1,000,000 (ex-primary residence) — statements (you may redact account #s).
  • Income ≥ $200k single / $300k joint — W-2s/1099s or returns for last two years.
  • Third-party (VerifyInvestor / Parallel Markets) confirmation letter.

Can’t share a link? Email files to verify@biarobotics.com. You may redact sensitive numbers.
