Accredited Investor Verification

Access to investment materials is restricted to verified accredited investors under SEC Regulation D. Submit one or more of the documents below. We review manually and reply from carl@biarobotics.com.

Acceptable documentation (choose one or more):

  • Letter from a CPA, attorney, or RIA confirming accredited status (preferred).
  • Financial statements (bank/brokerage) showing assets > $1,000,000 (excludes primary residence).
  • Income verification — W-2s/1099s or tax returns for the last two years (≥ $200k single / $300k joint).
  • Third-party verification (e.g., VerifyInvestor, Parallel Markets) — upload confirmation letter.

Tip: You may redact account numbers and non-essential details. You can upload files and/or share a secure link.

By submitting, you certify the information provided is accurate and you consent to our review for access to private offering materials.
